About Us

Started in 2013 ,leclog is an educational web application that sets out to make sharing of quality educational content among individuals and institutions , convenient and effective. Cutting across all works of life from the sciences to medicine , from sports to theology , leclog applies the simple "teacher and her students" metaphor with a little twist that anybody can be a teacher as well as a student anyone..
